Pop R&B Contemporary Vietnam · International

A mezzo-soprano voice trained at Vietnam's most prestigious music institutions, Châu Dương brings classical depth to a contemporary sound — crafting R&B and pop with rare vocal precision and emotional range. Based in Vietnam with an international performance footprint across Asia, Europe, North America, and Australia.
